About Dodgebot Game

At the ESD (Elite School of Dodgebot), Professor Jucibot leads another exciting day of lessons, teaching his students the rules and fundamentals of Dodgebot. To test their learning, he challenges two of his best students, b01 and b02, to a match. This game will reveal how well they have mastered the skills of dodging, defending, and counterattacking in a dynamic environment. With obstacles on the field and unpredictable ball movements, the stakes are high as they prepare to prove their capabilities. The challenge intensifies as players can defend against incoming balls, adding a layer of strategy and skill. The unpredictable nature of the obstacles and ball movements makes Dodgebot an exciting and fast-paced robotic twist on a classic game.
